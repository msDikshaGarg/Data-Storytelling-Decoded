The Market Mix

The housing market is diverse, but single-family homes dominate the landscape, representing the majority of available properties. Condos and townhouses offer alternative, often more affordable, entry points into the market.

This distribution is crucial for understanding inventory and demand dynamics across different segments.

Key Price Drivers

Driver 1: Size Matters

There is a strong, clear correlation between a home's square footage and its price. Larger homes consistently command higher prices, serving as the most fundamental indicator of value.

Driver 2: Location is Everything

Properties in neighborhoods with higher desirability scores (based on amenities, schools, etc.) are significantly more expensive. A top-tier location can more than double a property's value.

Driver 3: The Impact of Bedrooms

While overall size is key, the number of bedrooms is a critical factor for buyers. The data shows significant price jumps as bedroom counts increase, especially moving from two to three bedrooms, which hits a key demographic for families.

Market at a Glance

▲Strengths

High-value properties in desirable locations show strong market health. Consistent demand for 4 and 5-bedroom homes.

●Weaknesses

A significant portion of the housing stock was built before 2000, potentially requiring updates. Price growth for smaller homes is less aggressive.

■Opportunities

Older, well-located homes present significant value-add opportunities through renovation. Townhouses offer a growing, mid-market alternative.

▼Threats

Affordability is a major challenge in top-tier locations, potentially pricing out a large segment of buyers. High demand continues to push prices up.

Your Next Move

For Homebuyers

Focus on location and size to set your budget. Look for renovation opportunities in older homes to build equity. Use this data to make informed offers.

For Real Estate Agents

Leverage these insights to educate clients and set competitive prices. Highlight location scores and bedroom counts as key selling points in your listings.
